Who are we

Legacy Sports is a Canadian community sports organization dedicated to increasing participation in physical activity through inclusive recreational events and community-based initiatives. We organize structured tournaments, social sporting experiences, and wellness-focused programs designed to make sport accessible, welcoming, and enjoyable for individuals of all skill levels while bringing communities together through athletics.

Our Goals

Build a welcoming and inclusive sports community
Promote mental well-being through sport
Inspire passion, dreams, and lifelong participation in sport
Give back through volunteer opportunities and community involvement
Founder

Why I Started Legacy Sports

Legacy Sports was created from a belief that sport has the power to bring people together. Through my own experiences, I saw how athletics can build friendships, confidence, and a sense of belonging. I wanted to create a space where people could stay active, compete, and connect with others in a positive environment. Legacy Sports is about building community, creating meaningful experiences, and inspiring lifelong participation in sport.

Our Mission

Legacy Sports mission is to build an inclusive environment where individuals feel a true sense of belonging, stay active and build meaningful social connections. Through competitive and recreational events, we promote mental well-being, community, and experiences that leave a lasting impact.

Our Vision for the Community

We recognize that sport plays a powerful role in the lives of many Canadians. Some pursue greatness and a dream of becoming one of the best in their game. Others play simply for the joy of competition, to have fun with friends, or to stay active. For many, sport is an escape. A way to clear the mind, relieve stress, and feel mentally free. And for others, it’s about belonging and being part of something bigger and meeting new people. Our vision is to bring all of these individuals together in one inclusive space where everyone feels valued, connected, and inspired, no matter their reason for stepping onto the court.
