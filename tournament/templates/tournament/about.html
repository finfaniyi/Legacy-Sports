Who are we

Legacy Sports is a Canadian community sports organization focused on creating inclusive opportunities for individuals to thrive, stay active and connected through sports. We design and deliver structured tournaments and social sporting events that bring people together in a welcoming environment, while using sport as a way to support mental well-being and community connection.

Our Goals

Build a welcoming and inclusive sports community
Promote mental well-being through sport
Inspire passion, dreams, and lifelong participation in sport
Give back through volunteer opportunities and community involvement
Founder

Why I Started Legacy Sports

I was 14 when I moved to Canada and started high school. Everything felt new. At that age, it was overwhelming. I was trying to find my place, build friendships, and figure out where I belonged… and then COVID hit, making everything even more isolating. In the last year and a half of high school, things began to shift. I met people who showed me that you’re never truly alone, and that the right community can make all the difference. For the first time, I felt what it meant to be part of something. I have always loved sports and over time I explored others, such as volleyball and frisbee. Through sport, I found something I had been searching for. A way to connect. A way to belong. When I started university, I found myself back in that same uncertain place I was in Grade 9. But everything began to change when I joined intramurals and clubs. Through that, I met people who became real friends, and once again, I found a sense of community that changed everything. That is why I created Legacy Sports. Legacy Sports is built on a simple belief that sport has the power to bring people together. Not just through competition, but connection. It builds confidence, creates real friendships, and gives people a sense of belonging. You do not need to be an athlete to be part of it. It is not about skill or experience. It is about showing up, being included, and having a space where you belong. Legacy Sports is about more than just sport. It is about community, connection, and redefining what it means to be part of sport.

Our Mission

Legacy Sports mission is to build an inclusive environment where individuals feel a true sense of belonging, stay active and build meaningful social connections. Through competitive and recreational events, we promote mental well-being, community, and experiences that leave a lasting impact.

Our Vision for the Community

We recognize that sport plays a powerful role in the lives of many Canadians. Some pursue greatness and a dream of becoming one of the best in their game. Others play simply for the joy of competition, to have fun with friends, or to stay active. For many, sport is an escape. A way to clear the mind, relieve stress, and feel mentally free. And for others, it’s about belonging and being part of something bigger and meeting new people. Our vision is to bring all of these individuals together in one inclusive space where everyone feels valued, connected, and inspired, no matter their reason for stepping onto the court.
