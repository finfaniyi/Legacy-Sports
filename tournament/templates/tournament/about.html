Our Mission

Legacy Sports mission is to build an inclusive environment where individuals feel a true sense of belonging, stay active and build meaningful social connections. Through competitive and recreational events, we promote mental well-being, community, and experiences that leave a lasting impact.

Our Vision for the Community

We recognize that sport plays a powerful role in the lives of many Canadians. Some pursue greatness and a dream of becoming one of the best in their game. Others play simply for the joy of competition, to have fun with friends, or to stay active. For many, sport is an escape. A way to clear the mind, relieve stress, and feel mentally free. And for others, itâ€™s about belonging and being part of something bigger and meeting new people. Our vision is to bring all of these individuals together in one inclusive space where everyone feels valued, connected, and inspired, no matter their reason for stepping onto the court.
