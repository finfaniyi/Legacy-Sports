Tournament Format

Exhibition Round

All teams play 2 exhibition games to determine their seeding for elimination rounds.

Bracket

Teams are seeded 1–8 and placed into a single-elimination bracket.

Match Format

    Best of 3 sets
    Sets 1–2: First to 15 (No wins by two)
    Set 3: First to 11

Team Rules

Each team must have 6–8 players and at least 2 female players.
