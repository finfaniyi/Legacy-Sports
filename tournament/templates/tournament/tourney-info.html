Format

Structure

  • Single-elimination bracket (8 teams).
  • Pre-exhibition games to warm teams up.

Team Size

  • 6 players per team.

Matches

  • Best-of-3 short sets.
  • Sets 1 & 2: First to 15 points, win by 2.
  • Set 3 (if needed): First to 11 points, win by 2.

Fair Play

  • Refs and score tracking to keep matches fair.
