Format - MUST READ

Tournament Structure

  • To start the Tournament, Teams will be playing Exhibition games to put teams in seed based pots.
  • Rankings will be determined based on teams performances in their Exhibition round.
  • Brackets then will be set up based on teams ranking(1-8).
  • Once team rankings are set, It will be Single-elimination from then on.

Team Size

  • Each team will have a minimum of 6 players and a maximum of 8 players.
  • Teams have the choice to have 2 players as substituite.
  • This is Co-ed volleyball which means each team must have a minimum of 2 females per team
  • Failure to adhere to the rules will lead to a team forfeiting their spot

Matches

  • We will be running the Classic Best-of-3 sets.
  • Sets 1 & 2: First to 15 points, win by 2.
  • Set 3 (if needed): First to 11 points, win by 2.

Fair Play

  • Each match will have a referee and a scorekeeper to keep matches fair and unbiased.
