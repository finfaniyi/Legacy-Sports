Tournament Format

Tournament Structure

  • All teams will first play exhibition matches to determine tournament seeding.
  • Team rankings are based on performance during exhibition games.
  • Teams will be seeded from 1–8 and placed into the bracket.
  • After seeding, the tournament continues in a single-elimination format.

Team Requirements

  • Each team must have between 6 and 8 players.
  • Teams may include up to 2 substitute players.
  • This is a co-ed tournament.
  • Each team must have at least 2 female players.
  • Teams not meeting requirements may forfeit their spot.

Match Format

  • Matches are played best-of-3 sets.
  • Set 1 & 2: First to 15 points (win by 2).
  • Set 3: First to 11 points (win by 2).

Fair Play

  • Each match will have a referee.
  • A scorekeeper will track all points.
  • Sportsmanship is expected from all teams.
